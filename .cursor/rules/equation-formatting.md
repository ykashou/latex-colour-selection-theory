Important equations should be numbered and labeled for referencing.

Use:
```latex
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:identifier}
y = mx + b
\end{equation}
```

Reference with: `Equation~\ref{eq:identifier}`

For inline math: `$...$`
For unnumbered display math: `\[...\]`

Use `\text{...}` for words within equations: `$\Delta E_{\text{mean}}$`
